§ 00 — Foundations

Principles

Four rules, pinned
01

State is sacred.

Every surface makes the state of the pipeline visible at a glance: queued, running, stuck, passed, failed. Never decorative, never ambiguous.

02

Numerals are primary.

Durations, counts, IDs, diffs — data is the subject. Monospace dominates. Typography serves legibility at 11px before 64px.

03

Silence the chrome.

Hairline rules, warm black, restrained accents. The UI gets out of the way so the work — agents reasoning, code shipping — remains the loudest thing.

04

Motion signals progress.

We animate only when something is happening: a phase running, a ticket arriving, an agent thinking. Motion is information.

§ 07 — Voice

Tone & copy

Operator-grade · plain · no cheerleading
Do — operator voice
  • “Unit-02 failed 3/3. Escalated to team lead.”
  • “68% of reviewer findings upheld this week.”
  • “Needs clarification. Replied on HARN-2043.”
  • “Draft PR ready · 4 files · +142 −38.”
Don't — marketing voice
  • “Oops! Something went wrong 😬”
  • “Your AI teammates crushed it today! 🚀”
  • “We need a little more info to help you!”
  • “Ta-da! Your PR is ready to review ✨”
